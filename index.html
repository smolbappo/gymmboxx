    About GYMMBOXX

    Established in October 2010, GYMMBOXX started as a single gym situated at the top of a multi-story car park in Singapore (Bedok Reservoir). Over the years, GYMMBOXX has made its mark as Singapore’s affordable premium gym brand, serving the residential neighborhoods and heartlands. With more than 6 gyms (and counting) island-wide, GYMMBOXX will continue to expand and bring quality gym facilities and service to the rest of Singapore and beyond.
